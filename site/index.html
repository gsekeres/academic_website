Gabe Sekeres

I am a second-year PhD student in the Department of Economics at Cornell University. I am a microeconomic theorist working with Marco Battaglini on problems in political economy, with Nic Fishman on metascience, and on my own on optimal mechanism design with applications to AI agents.

Prior to Cornell, I was a predoctoral research fellow at Stanford University, working with Laurence Baker and Eric Sun. I hold a B.A. in Economics and Mathematics from Haverford College. My CV is here.
